# Basics
WARSHIPS is a naval combat game. Start with a small ship, collect crates to level up, and sink enemy ships to earn upgrades to bigger, more powerful warships.

# Movement
There are several ways to move your ship:
1. **Tap and hold** on the screen to set direction and speed (on touch screen)
2. **Left click and hold** to set direction (on desktop)
3. Use **WASD** or **arrow keys** to move, and 'x' key to stop

Your ship will keep moving in a direction once set, so you can focus on combat.

# Ships
There are many types of ships, each with different strengths:

1. **Patrol Boats** — small, fast, and nimble. Great for beginners.
2. **Destroyers** — medium ships with torpedoes and guns.
3. **Cruisers** — balanced between speed and firepower.
4. **Battleships** — massive firepower with powerful main cannons.
5. **Submarines** — travel underwater, immune to some weapons.
6. **Aircraft Carriers** — command a squadron of aircraft to attack enemies!

Earn points to unlock bigger ships via the **upgrade** menu at the top of the screen.

See all ships: [Ships](/ships/).

# Weapons
Fire weapons by clicking or tapping in the direction you want to shoot:

1. **Torpedoes** — powerful underwater weapons. Some track targets automatically.
2. **Missiles** — fast airborne weapons.
3. **Gun Turrets** — rapid-fire shells with moderate damage.
4. **Depth Charges** — drop behind you to hit pursuing ships or submarines.
5. **Aircraft** — fly toward your target and attack automatically.

Weapons take time to **reload** after firing. Collecting crates speeds up reloading.

# Sensors
Ships have sensors to detect enemies:
1. **Visual** — sees targets based on conditions.
2. **Radar** — tracks targets above water.
3. **Sonar** — tracks underwater targets.

Toggle between **active** and **passive** sensor modes with the 'z' key. Active mode sees more but reveals your position.
